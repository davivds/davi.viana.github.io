About Me

Associate Professor at the Federal University of Maranhão (UFMA), involved in undergraduate and graduate education (PPGCC & DCCMAPI). I hold a Ph.D. and a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Graduate Program in Computer Science at the Federal University of Amazonas (UFAM, Brazil). I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from UFAM and also have a technical high school diploma in computer science from the Nokia Teaching Foundation. My research interests include Software Engineering, Software Quality, Software Process Improvement (SPI), Empirical Software Engineering, Information Systems, and Computing Education.
